is the 12th main title in the Assassin's Creed series, developed by Ubisoft and released in November 2020. The game is set at the end of the 9th century and is set in the Viking Age, offering a rich historical and mythological experience. .

Plot

Eivor Varinsdottir

The game follows Eivor Varinsdottir, a Viking warrior who seeks to conquer and explore new lands in England during the Age of the Vikings. Eivor, who can be played as a man or a woman, is the leader of a Viking clan who leaves Norway due to internal conflict and seeks to establish a new home in the British lands

History

In "Assassin's Creed Valhalla", you take on the role of Eivor Varinsdottir, a courageous Viking warrior from Norway in the late 9th century. After a series of tragedies that devastate their homeland, Eivor and her clan set out in search of a new home, sailing long boats towards England, a land of promise and challenge. Upon arriving in England, Eivor finds a country divided into warring kingdoms, such as Wessex and Mercia, who fight for control of the lands. With the desire to establish a new settlement for his people, Eivor finds himself immersed in conflicts with the Saxons, in addition to having to deal with political intrigues and local rivalries. However, Eivor's journey goes beyond simply conquering land. He enters into a hidden conflict between two secret factions: the Assassins, who seek freedom and knowledge, and the Templars, who wish to control and impose order. This underground conflict between the two factions forms a crucial part of the story, influencing Eivor's decisions and actions. Norse mythology plays a central role in the plot. Eivor interacts with mythical figures such as Odin and Loki and faces challenges that reflect the legends and beliefs of the ancient Vikings. These encounters with the supernatural world offer an extra dimension to your adventure and deeply impact your journey. In addition to battles and alliances, Eivor is tasked with building and expanding the Viking settlement. This aspect of the game allows you to customize and develop the settlement, recruiting new members and improving the infrastructure to ensure the success and prosperity of the clan. "Assassin's Creed Valhalla" offers a rich and immersive experience, mixing real history with Norse mythology. Through exploration of a vast open world, intense combat, and impactful decisions, Eivor seeks to not only conquer new lands, but also build a lasting legacy for her clan and confront the mysteries that shape their destiny
